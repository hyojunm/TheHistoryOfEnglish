    • Changing for Fifteen Centuries

    English dates back to 15-16 centuries ago in the land we now know as England. Since its first use, English has been constantly changing, by other countries, new inventions, important events, and more. Even writers like William Shakespeare did a part in developing the language. In this website you will learn how English developed to be the world's most spoken global language.
