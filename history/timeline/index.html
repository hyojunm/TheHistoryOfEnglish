    • English History Timeline of Important Events

    449 - 6th century

    Angles, Saxons, and Jutes cross the North Sea and invade Britain, forcing out all Celtic-speaking natives.

    597

    St. Augustine arrive to Britain and Latin Christianity is introduced.

    878

    Norse-speaking Vikings invade Britain.

    1066

    William the Conqueror from the Normandy battle the Anglo-Saxon Britain and wins; Normandy takes over Britain and French becomes official language.
