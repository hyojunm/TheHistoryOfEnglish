    • About 20% Time

    This website is a part of my 20% Time Project. 20% Time is where we spend 20% of the school week — or once a week, usually Fridays — researching and learning about a topic we want to learn about. We researched by looking at databases, websites, and also by conducting an interview with an expert. At the end, we presented our learnings to others with a short 3-minute Pecha Kucha.

    To learn more about this project, visit the Googlio 20% Time page.
